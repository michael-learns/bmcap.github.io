Membership at BMCAP

Be part of a trusted network of professional business and management consultants in the Philippines. The Business and Management Consultants Association of the Philippines (BMCAP) offers a collaborative and enriching community committed to advancing ethical, excellent, and impactful consulting practices across industries.

Why Join BMCAP?

  • Engage with a dynamic network of consultants across diverse fields.
  • Gain access to mentorship, collaborative projects, and professional development events.
  • Receive invitations to learning sessions, networking events, and General Assemblies.
  • Contribute to the advancement and credibility of the consulting profession.
  • Uphold the BMCAP Code of Ethics and Rules of Engagement.

Membership Categories

1. Associate Member

Requirements: Interest or initial experience in consulting, with commitment to earn certification within one year.

Benefits: Access to workshops, mentoring, and networking events; One-year assessment with support for advancement.

2. Regular Member

Requirements: One year as Associate Member in good standing; Certified in PGC-BMC, CMC, CPA, RFP, or equivalent.

Benefits: Full voting rights, leadership eligibility, and priority access to BMCAP programs and initiatives.

3. Sponsored Member

Eligibility: Endorsed by a high-performing BMCAP member or graduate of a recognized certification program.

Benefits: 50% discount on membership fees and full access to Associate-level opportunities.

4. Honorary Member

Eligibility: Nominated individuals with exceptional contributions to BMCAP or the consulting profession.

Privileges: Full rights, including voting and leadership eligibility, without membership fees.

Eligibility and Application Requirements

  • Completed Membership Application Form
  • Updated Resume/CV
  • Proof of Certification (for Regular Member applicants)
  • Endorsement Letters (one to three, depending on membership type)
  • Government-issued ID and recent 2x2 photo
  • Signed Conflict of Interest Declaration
  • Attendance at a Vision Casting and Orientation Session

Membership Fees

  • Annual Fee: PHP 5,000
  • New Members: Pay within 30 days of acceptance
  • Renewals: Due before January 31 yearly
  • Reinstatement: PHP 500 late renewal fee
  • Note: Fees are non-refundable except for administrative errors.

Engagement & Participation

Members must maintain active participation through:

  • Annual General Assembly attendance
  • Minimum of 20 engagement points annually
  • Compliance with BMCAP's Code of Ethics and Rules of Engagement

How to Apply

  1. Download and complete the BMCAP Membership Application Form
  2. Prepare all supporting documents
  3. Submit to the BMCAP Membership Committee
  4. Attend interview (if required)
  5. Participate in orientation and take the BMCAP Oath
  6. Receive approval and pay membership dues

Ethical Commitment

All members pledge to uphold the BMCAP Code of Ethics, emphasizing integrity, confidentiality, professional competence, and responsibility. The Code ensures high standards of practice and guides ethical decision-making across all engagements.
