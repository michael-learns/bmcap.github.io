About BMCAP

BMCAP is a non-stock, non-profit, non-sectarian, and non-political professional association formed by the 1st batch of graduates of AIM-SEELL's Postgraduate Certification in Business and Management Consulting. We promote industry-wide excellence by upholding ethical practice, mentoring future consultants, and setting professional standards across the Philippine consulting landscape.

Our Vision

To be a highly trusted group of dynamic and professional business and management consultants, creating positive change and impact within and across industries.

Our Mission

  • Share best practices across diverse consulting fields in pursuit of professional excellence.
  • Provide opportunities for networking, training, and lifelong learning to foster collaboration.
  • Act as an advocate and catalyst for innovation, service excellence, and ethical standards in the consulting profession.

Ethical Leadership

Guided by our Code of Ethics, we prioritize transparency, objectivity, and integrity in all engagements.

Collaboration & Mentorship

We foster knowledge-sharing and peer learning through industry linkages and internal mentorship.

Lifelong Learning

We champion professional development with continuous training, certifications, and research initiatives.

Our Core Values

Trust

We foster a positive and supportive environment within the association, promoting effective communication, collaboration, and mutual respect among our members.

Service

We are committed and dedicated to meeting the needs and expectations of our members, stakeholders, and the community we serve. Within our association, we put the interests and well-being of others before personal gain and strive for excellence in delivering quality experiences, programs, and initiatives.

Integrity

We value each other's reliability and competence. We expect each member will act in good faith, fulfill their commitments, and maintain confidentiality when necessary.

Diversity

We respect each other's fields of expertise and recognize the value each brings to the industry's development. We are united in our uniqueness.

Agility

We quickly adapt to a constantly changing environment and respond to the needs of our clients and colleagues without compromising quality. We continuously seek opportunities for professional growth and development.

What's New at BMCAP

The SME Lab: A Strategic Collaboration with Malaya Business Insight

BMCAP, is honored to collaborate with Malaya Business Insight in presenting The SME Lab—a dedicated column designed to empower small and medium enterprises (SMEs) throughout the Philippines. This initiative reflects our shared commitment to provide actionable, experience-based insights that drive growth and resilience in the SME sector.

The SME Lab serves as a platform where BMCAP consultants contribute practical guidance on key business challenges such as digital transformation, operational efficiency, and strategic planning. Drawing from decades of combined experience, our contributors aim to support SME leaders in navigating complex market realities with clarity and confidence.

This partnership is rooted in mutual trust and a shared vision of fostering informed decision-making and sustainable development among Filipino enterprises. Through this collaboration, BMCAP enhances Malaya Business Insight's role as a source of business intelligence, while reinforcing our commitment to serve as enablers of economic progress.
